How it works

Four steps. Zero friction.

1 📷

Scan

Guest scans the QR code on their table or in their room. The menu opens instantly — no app.

2 🍽️

Order

They browse your live menu with photos and order. It lands with the kitchen and waiter in real time.

3 💳

Pay

M-Pesa prompt on their phone, card, or cash — money goes straight to your paybill or till.

4 🛋️

Relax

They track their order live and get a receipt. Your staff serve more tables with less running.
